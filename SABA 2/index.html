Our Mission

What we are building, and why it matters.

The South Asian Business Association at Cornell University aims to provide students with business and leadership skills through a comprehensive education process. We empower our students to explore business initiatives via mentorship from campus leaders, exclusive speaker events hosted by notable South Asian alumni, and membership in a community of lifelong friends.
